# Safety Protocols Reference

> Reference for the `electrical_safety_check` tool and Quality Gate G3.
> Grounded in IEC 60364-7-702 and SECOND-KNOWLEDGE-BRAIN.md Section 1.1.5.

## Electrical Safety Near Water (IEC 60364-7-702)

- Any circuit within 3 m of the water edge requires RCD/GFCI protection
  (trip <= 30 mA, response <= 40 ms).
- Galvanic isolation required for circuits within 3 m of water.
- All junction boxes near water must be IP-rated per IEC 60529.
- Annual PAT (Portable Appliance Testing) for all stage equipment.
- Emergency power cutoff accessible to the stage manager.

## Operator Safety

- Non-slip surfaces at the operator station.
- Water quality monitoring (bacterial + chemical).
- Maximum 4-hour continuous operation in water; rotate operators.
- Documented emergency egress plan.

## Audience Safety & Accessibility

- Barrier at minimum safe distance from the water edge.
- Fire exits unobstructed and illuminated.
- Fog/haze density monitored (visibility >= safe threshold).
- Wheelchair-accessible viewing positions.
- Audio description capability and tactile tour options.

## Pre-Effects Rule (G3)

A safety plan (electrical + operator) MUST be present and validated BEFORE any
physical/visual effects (fog, projection, submerged lighting) are specified.
